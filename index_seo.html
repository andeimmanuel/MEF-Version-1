Volunteer With Us

Join our global community of changemakers and help create lasting impact in underserved communities.

Why Volunteer With Us?

Volunteering with The Mary Emmanuel Foundation offers a unique opportunity to make a meaningful difference while developing valuable skills and connections.

Make a Real Impact

Our volunteers work directly with communities, seeing firsthand how their contributions create positive change in people's lives.

Join a Global Community

Connect with like-minded individuals from around the world who share your passion for creating a more equitable world.

Develop New Skills

Gain valuable experience, enhance your resume, and develop both professional and personal skills through meaningful service.

Volunteer Opportunities

We offer a variety of ways to get involved, both in-person and remotely, across our program areas.

Education
Remote In-Person

Education Support

Help with tutoring, curriculum development, teaching English, computer skills training, and educational resource creation.

Time Commitment: 3-10 hours/week

Requirements: Teaching experience preferred but not required

Healthcare
In-Person

Healthcare Assistance

Support medical outreach programs, health education initiatives, clinic operations, and community health worker training.

Time Commitment: 5-20 hours/week

Requirements: Healthcare background for clinical roles

Advocacy
Remote In-Person

Community Outreach

Assist with community organizing, awareness campaigns, advocacy initiatives, and community needs assessments.

Time Commitment: 2-8 hours/week

Requirements: Strong communication skills

Operations
Remote

Administrative Support

Help with grant writing, social media management, graphic design, data analysis, translation, and general administrative tasks.

Time Commitment: 2-15 hours/week

Requirements: Relevant professional skills

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about volunteering with The Mary Emmanuel Foundation.

Requirements vary by role, but generally we look for individuals who are at least 18 years old, have relevant skills or a willingness to learn, and can commit to a minimum time period (typically 3 months). Some specialized roles may require specific qualifications or experience. All volunteers must complete our application process and orientation program.

Yes! We have many remote volunteer opportunities that can be done from anywhere in the world. These include roles in grant writing, social media management, translation, curriculum development, graphic design, and more. Remote volunteers are a vital part of our global team and contribute significantly to our mission.

Our application review process typically takes 5-7 business days. If your application is selected, you'll be invited for an interview (in-person or virtual). The entire process from application to placement usually takes 2-3 weeks, though this may vary depending on the role and current demand.

For long-term international volunteers (3+ months), we can assist with finding affordable housing options and may provide housing stipends for certain roles. Short-term volunteers are generally responsible for their own accommodations, though we can provide recommendations. All housing arrangements are discussed during the interview process.

Yes, all volunteers receive a comprehensive orientation to our organization and specific training for their role. The training format varies by position but may include online modules, in-person workshops, shadowing experienced volunteers, and ongoing mentorship. We're committed to ensuring all volunteers feel prepared and supported in their roles.
