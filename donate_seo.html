Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about donating to The Mary Emmanuel Foundation.

Yes, The Mary Emmanuel Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. You will receive a tax receipt for your records.

92% of every donation goes directly to our programs in the field. This includes education initiatives, healthcare services, community advocacy, and economic empowerment programs. The remaining 8% is used for administrative costs and fundraising efforts to sustain our work.

Yes, you can set up a monthly recurring donation. This provides consistent support for our programs and helps us plan effectively for the future. You can cancel or modify your recurring donation at any time by contacting our donor services team.

Yes, you can designate your donation to a specific program area such as education, healthcare, advocacy, or economic empowerment. You can also choose "Where Needed Most" to allow us to direct your donation to the areas of greatest need.

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your personal and payment information. We never store your full credit card details on our servers. All transactions are processed through secure payment gateways that comply with PCI DSS standards.
